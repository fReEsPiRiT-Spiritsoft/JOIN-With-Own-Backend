If you contact me via email or contact form, the personal data you voluntarily provide (e.g., name, email address, message) will be stored for the purpose of processing your inquiry.

4. Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve the user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Some are necessary for the website to function, while others help analyze usage or remember preferences.

You can prevent cookies from being stored by adjusting your browser settings; however, this may limit some website functions.
